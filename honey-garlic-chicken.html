Honey Garlic Chicken with Jasmine Rice and Broccoli

Yield: 6 servings

Calories per serving: 900

Protein: 58g | Carbs: 118g | Fat: 12g

Ingredients

  • 1130g chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 555g white jasmine rice, uncooked
  • 600g broccoli florets
  • 110g honey
  • 80ml low-sodium soy sauce
  • 45ml rice vinegar
  • 12g garlic, minced (about 4 cloves)
  • 15g fresh ginger, grated
  • 16g cornstarch
  • 60ml water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Cook jasmine rice according to package directions. Season and cook chicken in batches until golden and cooked through. Whisk together honey, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water. Pour sauce into pan, stir until thickened, return chicken and toss to coat. Steam or roast broccoli until tender-crisp. Portion rice, chicken, and broccoli into meal prep containers.
