High-Protein Breakfast Burritos

Yield: 6 servings

Calories per serving: 360

Protein: 30g | Carbs: 42g | Fat: 7g

Ingredients

  • 475g liquid egg whites
  • 450g low-fat cottage cheese (1% or 2%)
  • 450g lean ground turkey (93/7)
  • 150g bell pepper, diced
  • 100g onion, diced
  • 30g fresh spinach, chopped
  • 425g black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 6 large whole wheat tortillas (about 360g total)
  • 3g garlic powder
  • 3g ground cumin
  • 3g chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Cook ground turkey with seasonings until browned. Sauté vegetables until softened. Scramble egg whites with cottage cheese until just set. Assemble burritos by layering eggs, turkey mixture, and black beans in tortillas. Fold and wrap tightly.
