High-Protein Breakfast Burritos

Yield: 6 servings

Calories per serving: 360

Protein: 30g | Carbs: 42g | Fat: 7g

Ingredients

  • 16 oz liquid egg whites
  • 16 oz low-fat cottage cheese (1% or 2%)
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey (93/7)
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 15 oz black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 6 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Cook ground turkey with seasonings until browned. Sauté vegetables until softened. Scramble egg whites with cottage cheese until just set. Assemble burritos by layering eggs, turkey mixture, and black beans in tortillas. Fold and wrap tightly.
