Creamy Mushroom Beef Pasta with Green Beans

Yield: 6 servings

Calories per serving: 900

Protein: 60g | Carbs: 118g | Fat: 18g

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef (93/7)
  • 18 oz Barilla Protein pasta
  • 1 lb mushrooms, sliced
  • 1.5 lbs fresh green beans
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups beef broth, low sodium
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup heavy cream or half-and-half
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder for green beans

Instructions

Brown ground beef with salt and pepper, drain and set aside. Cook mushrooms until golden, add onion and garlic until softened. Return beef to pan, sprinkle with flour and stir. Add broth, Worcestershire, mustard, thyme, and rosemary. Simmer until slightly thickened. Stir in cream and Parmesan. Cook pasta according to package directions. Steam or roast green beans with garlic powder. Portion pasta, beef sauce, and green beans into meal prep containers.
